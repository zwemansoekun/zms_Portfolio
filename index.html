About Me

I studied Petroleum Engineering at West Yangon Technology University but left the school in the second year of the B.Tech program, receiving only an A.G.T.I Diploma. There was no IT training at the university.

  • Name: Zwe Man Soe
  • Age: 33
  • Occupation: Web Developer

I learned programming at G-Next Myanmar Company, a branch of a Japanese company, and worked in the Android department there for about a year and a half. Afterward, I served as a PHP developer at Operation Partners Myanmar Company, a branch office of a Japanese company, for approximately 4 and a half years. Following that, I took on the role of Flutter Team Leader at Management Partners Myanmar Company, a branch of a Japanese company. In my latest position, I joined CGM Golden Land Company, also a branch of a Japanese company, as a BSE. I primarily functioned as an Admin Team Leader and Marketing Leader in the office. Furthermore, I have successfully passed the N2 level of the Nat-Test Japanese language exam.
